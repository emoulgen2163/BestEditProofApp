# User Personas

This document defines user personas for the Best Edit & Proof Android App.  
Currently, only the **Client User** persona is included as requested.

---

## **1. Client User Persona**

### **Name:** Sarah Thompson (Representative Academic Client)
### **Age:** 22–40
### **Profession:** University student, graduate researcher, PhD candidate, or academic professional
### **Location:** Global (USA, UK, Europe, Middle East, Asia)

---

## **Background & Demographics**
- Often working under strict academic deadlines
- Frequently writes essays, theses, dissertations, research papers, journal manuscripts, or scholarship applications
- Non‑native English speakers make up a large portion of users
- Comfortable using mobile apps for productivity and file management

---

## **Goals & Motivations**
- Submit well‑polished academic documents with correct grammar, clarity, and formatting
- Receive reliable feedback from expert editors
- Track the status of editing orders without checking email constantly
- Upload documents easily from mobile devices (Word, PDF, etc.)
- Get quick turnaround options for urgent assignments

---

## **Pain Points / Challenges**
- Struggles with academic writing quality or English as a second language
- Tight deadlines and high pressure
- Difficulty finding qualified editors they can trust
- Confusion with formatting or structure requirements (APA, MLA, Chicago, etc.)
- Wants a transparent pricing and order‑tracking system

---

## **Why They Use the App**
- Mobile convenience: place orders anytime, especially during last‑minute editing needs
- Clear order process and transparent pricing
- Ability to track progress rather than waiting for emails
- Quick communication if revisions are needed
- Easy retrieval of past edited documents

---

## **Key App Behaviors**
- Logs in primarily to check order status or place new orders
- Frequently uploads academic files directly from phone storage or cloud services
- Prefers simple, clean UI with minimal steps
- Responds well to notifications (order updates, completion alerts)
